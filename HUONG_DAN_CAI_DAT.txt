CAI DAT LAN DAU — KHONG CAN INTERNET (phuong phap moi)
-------------------------------------------------------
Dieu kien: Thu muc co san "python_embed\" (da duoc chuan bi san).

1. Copy toan bo thu muc sang may tinh dich
   (vi du: D:\PhanLichTruc\)

2. Nhan dup-click vao file  start.bat
   - Tu dong su dung Python portable trong python_embed\
   - KHONG CAN cai Python tren may
   - KHONG CAN internet

3. Trinh duyet tu dong mo giao dien tai: http://localhost:8081


CHUAN BI GOI OFFLINE (chi can lam 1 lan, tren may CO INTERNET)
---------------------------------------------------------------
Neu chua co thu muc "python_embed\", can chuan bi tren may co internet:

1. Copy thu muc chuong trinh vao may co internet

2. Chay file  setup_embed_python.bat
   - Tu dong tai Python 3.11.9 portable (~25 MB)
   - Cai tat ca thu vien can thiet
   - Tao thu muc python_embed\

3. Copy toan bo thu muc (gio da co python_embed\) sang may dich
   va chay start.bat binh thuong.


SU DUNG HANG NGAY
------------------
- Nhan dup-click vao  start.bat
- Cho den khi trinh duyet tu dong mo (khoang 30-60 giay)
- De tat: nhan Enter trong cua so "Phan lich truc"


CAU TRUC THU MUC
-----------------
  start.bat                <- FILE CHAY CHINH
  setup_embed_python.bat   <- Chuan bi goi offline (may co internet)
  launcher.py              <- Script khoi dong
  python_embed\            <- Python 3.11 portable (tu dong dung, khong can cai)
  packages\                <- Thu vien du phong (whl)
  database\                <- Co so du lieu SQLite
  backend\                 <- FastAPI server (port 8001)
  frontend\                <- NiceGUI UI (port 8081)


XU LY SU CO
-----------
- Neu trinh duyet khong tu mo: Mo thu cong tai http://localhost:8081
- Neu bao loi "Python not found": Chay setup_embed_python.bat
  tren may co internet de tao python_embed\
- Neu co loi khac: Xem chi tiet trong cua so Backend / Frontend
